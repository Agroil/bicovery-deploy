Fintech UX Design Trends for 2021

UI and UX design in fintech specifics is largely determined by the industry trends that should be wisely followed when creating your mobile or web application for finances. Below are the most prominent fintech web design trends for 2021.

Gamification. Gamification features are great for making the user experience with financial apps really enjoyable. For example, setting spending limits for a month may become an interesting challenge instead of torture if to approach this idea wisely.

Human-centered copy and microcopy. Modern users want fintech startups to communicate with them in human language and explain financial terms as well as your values with simple words.

Mobile-first services. In 2021, the UX design for fintech should be centered around the opportunity to perform all the necessary tasks within one app while keeping UI as simple as possible.

Strong brand identity. 2020 has become a year of opportunities for financial startups as the number of financial operations performed online, as well as the number of users, has increased significantly under the influence of the pandemic. In a highly competitive environment, your brand identity comes first. Thus, your brand style elements should be clearly reflected in your fintech graphic design.

User Experience Design and Fintech - Main Essentials to Keep in Mind

  • Psychology of colors. Colors are quite important in a fintech website design since they are the main visual impression makers. Thus, the colors you choose for your app should promote trust and credibility. For example, PayPal uses white and blue colors which are the colors of stability and reliability.
  • Safety. Safety features also matter for fintech UI design. The user should feel comfortable with the necessary authentications and security checks, and simple still reliable design should contribute to the feeling of safety.
  • Legal regulations. Also, there are a lot of legal requirements for fintech design for a bank or other financial organization. For example, some of the positions of the PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) Compliance Checklist dwell on the UI as well.
